description

Clicker Heroes is a fun idle game. Your mission is to defeat monsters and many mythical creatures along the journey. Earn gold, upgrade your heroes and unlock new satisfying skills to help you kill monsters faster.

Features of the game:

- Unrestricted progression: Jump from zone to zone to kill monsters, collect loot, and buy gear and upgrades that will enable you proceed to higher zones and unlock even greater improvements. When you've gained enough power, move on to the next realm, where you'll encounter even more powerful enemies and wonderful loot!

- Skills: Unlock a plethora of rewarding skills to aid in the faster killing of creatures. Big Clicks, Huge Clicks, Multiclicks, and other options are available. For optimal DPS, find the ideal combination and rotation!

- Planets: Conquer a large number of distinct worlds, each with its own unique creatures, in search of ever-increasing amounts of wealth!

- Automator: Set up the automator to play the game while you're away. Level up your gear, buy new gear, and construct intricate skill combos automatically. Make the most of your time by creating the ideal automator sequences.

- Massive Skill Tree: Choose your own route to success by upgrading your stats and skills with hundreds of incredible improvements in the massive skill tree.

instruction

To kill the monsters and obtain their gold, click on them. Use your gold to hire new heroes and increase your damage. The more damage you strike, the more gold you get.
